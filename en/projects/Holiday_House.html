Name HOLIDAY HOUSE
Location Santa Teresa, Costa Rica
Program Vacation home
Area 240 m2

21125 EBAUCHE VILLA copie _ 1. R+1.jpg

The site of this family vacation home is a clearing with a spectacular slope of nearly 20m plunging into the jungle. perched on top of the hill, a canopy roof opens to the north and west on the vast landscape and tree tops as far as the eye can see. It houses a base divided into three platforms following the natural terrain topography and is designed in continuity with the outdoor decks and the infinity pool. The only tree on the site is maintained and fully integrated into the heart of the living space. The leveling work of the base was carried out to optimize the roof: the three suites are stacked to enjoy the same spectacular view sheltered by retractable shutters and a double-oriented cathedral volume is released to accommodate a living space with vertical circulations that can be seen while an office space is installed on a mezzanine in the background. Upon arriving from the access easement, a large hermetic screen facade ensures the privacy of the occupants and participates in the mystery: nothing reveals such an opening and spaces deliberately erasing the boundaries of inside and outside.
